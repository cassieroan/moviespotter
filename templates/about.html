Welcome to MovieSpotters!


We're your destination for movie enthusiasts and avid city explorers alike! Our site is dedicated to helping you track and log movie filming posters scattered throughout New York City.

Join our community today and embark on a thrilling cinematic scavenger hunt across the Big Apple!

Add us to your phone's home screen for quick access! ⬇️

1. Go to https://moviespotters.com/submit in Safari

2. While viewing the submit page, tap the button in the middle of the menu bar at the bottom of your screen.

3. Scroll down the list of options, then tap “Add to Home Screen ”

4. Once you’re on the “Add to Home Screen” page, click the “Add” button on the top right of the screen.

And you’re done— it’s as simple as that! 🚀
