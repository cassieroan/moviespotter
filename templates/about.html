About the Movement


Welcome to MovieSpotters! We are your ultimate destination for movie enthusiasts and avid city explorers alike. Our site is dedicated to helping you track and log movie filming posters scattered throughout New York City.

At MovieSpotters, we believe that every movie poster tells a unique story, just like the city itself. Our user-friendly interface lets you effortlessly log and geotag your discoveries, creating a virtual map of movie magic. Not only can you document your sightings, but you can also interact with fellow film enthusiasts, sharing your experiences, and collaborating on uncovering the latest movie promotions.

Whether you're a passionate movie buff or simply a curious wanderer, join our community today and embark on a thrilling cinematic scavenger hunt across the Big Apple!
